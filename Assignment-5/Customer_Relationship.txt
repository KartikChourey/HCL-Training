1. Abstract

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is an essential software system used by organizations to manage customer interactions, improve customer satisfaction, and enhance business growth. Traditional customer data management methods are often manual, time-consuming, and error-prone. To overcome these challenges, this project focuses on developing a CRM system using Java technology and the Agile software development methodology.
